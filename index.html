About Us
Greenery Mart Corporation is a retail and wholesale distribution company that started its operation in Ibajay, Aklan in the year 2000, emphasizing on selling fast moving consumer goods (FMCG) at low prices.

Now, Greenery Mart Corporation caters to a population of around 1.2 million people, with a distribution area spanning 24 municipalities throughout the Panay island region.
